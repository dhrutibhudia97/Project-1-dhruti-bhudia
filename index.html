How stress can affect your health

  • Stress can vary from small inconveniences at work to huge problems that can affect many aspects of your lives.
  • Stress can have huge effects on your health, from getting headaches, chest pain to weakening our immune system and making us ill.
  • We cant avoid stress, but how we deal with it can help us improve our well being in the long run.
  • A few small changes in your daily routine that require little effort can have a noticeable impact right away on your well being.
